About Applied Language Resources

This project is a resource for researchers of Linguistics and language students. Researchers may collect and View Audio through this website. The data is collected from native language speakers. Native speakers score student audio recordings based on how native-like their speech is. Students may access this resource in order to listen to themselves, view feedback, and practice their speech. Please click on the "Sign Up" button in order to apply for an account.
